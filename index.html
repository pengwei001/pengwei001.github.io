Peng Wei

Starting from August 2019, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Global Energy Interconnection Research Institute North America (GEIRINA). My research interests center around the analysis and development of high-performance computation of transient-state simulation in the modern power system.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Applied Mathematics from Texas A&M University under supervision of Dr. Andrea Bonito. My dissertation is entitled Approximation for Time Dependent Fractional Diffusion with Drift and it's Applications to Surface Quasi-Geostrophic Dynamics and Electroconvection Systems.
